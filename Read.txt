KAFKA TUTORIALS

PRODUCER -> MESSAGE BROKER -> CONSUMER

KAFKA COMPONENTS:

1) Producer
2) Consumer
3) Broker  --> Kafka server
4) Cluster ---> group of kafka brokers
5) Topic   ---> It specify the category of the message or classfication of the message.
6) Partitions --> splitting the topic to the partitions, if any partition goes off, other will work.
7) Offset --> we will give the indices to the message in partions. It will keep the track, what message been sent and what not.
8) Consumer Groups --> Group of consumer to achieve the better output.
9) Zookeeper --> It will keep track of topic, offset, broker everything.
